# Privacy

Marty Authenticator stores wallet data and credentials on the user's device and
communicates with credential issuers, verifiers, and other services selected by
the user or the user's deployment. Those external services have their own data
practices and privacy policies.

Diagnostic logs can contain technical details about app activity. Review them
before choosing to share them in a support request. Do not include credentials,
private keys, recovery material, or other sensitive personal information in a
public issue.

For security-sensitive reports, use the repository's private vulnerability
reporting feature. For privacy questions, open a repository discussion or use
the support contact published by the organization deploying the app.
